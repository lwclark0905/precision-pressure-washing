About Us

Precision Pressure Washing is a locally veteran owned business proudly serving Anderson, Greenville, and the surrounding Upstate South Carolina area. Founded by Landon Eudy, Logan Clark, and Carson Wyatt, we are lifelong locals committed to honest work, reliable service, and top-tier results.

With backgrounds in mechanical work, firefighting, and hands-on trades, our team brings strong attention to detail and professionalism to every job. We specialize in residential and commercial pressure washing, offering free estimates and referral discounts you can trust.
